Select each card to flip and reveal the answer.

James is on the football team only. There's a custom group for football team members. James gets removed from the football team. What happens to his group membership?

James is removed from the custom group — his membership was tied to his football team affiliation.

Kevin is on football AND basketball. The "Captains" group spans both teams. Kevin is removed from football only. Is he removed from Captains?

No — Kevin stays in Captains because he's still on basketball. He'd only be removed if removed from both teams.

A custom group uses the "All Teams" option. An athlete is removed from one of their teams. Are they removed from the group?

No — All Teams groups include users across all teams. Removal from one team doesn't affect membership unless they leave all teams.

A custom group spans Team A and Team B. A new athlete joins Team A. Are they automatically added to the group?

Yes — if the group was built using the athlete user type, new athletes added to Team A are automatically included.
