Concept 1
Hub Position vs Custom Position
Click to learn →
What's the difference?
Hub positions sync automatically from Teamworks Hub. Custom positions are created inside GM to match your unique team structure — and they persist across academic years.
Concept 2
How to change an athlete's position
Click to learn →
The steps
Select the athlete from the roster table → open the side panel → click the position field → choose from the Hub list or type a new name and select Create → click Change Position.
Concept 3
Apply to current year vs all upcoming years
Click to learn →
When to use each
Choose "current year only" for a temporary adjustment. Choose "all upcoming years" when the position change reflects your long-term roster structure for that athlete.
Concept 4
Does changing a position in GM affect Hub?
Click to learn →
Important to know
No — changing an athlete's position in GM (especially to a custom position) does not update their listed position in Teamworks Hub. The two systems are independent for position data.
