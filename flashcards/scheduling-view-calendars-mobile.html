Dashboard
When you open the Hub mobile app, today's events appear directly on the dashboard — giving you an at-a-glance view of your schedule without navigating anywhere.

Navigation
Scroll up or down to move through dates, or use the left and right arrow buttons to jump between days. Tap any date in the mini-calendar at the top to jump directly to it.
Feature 3
Viewing another user's calendar
Click to learn →
Other Users
Tap the name selector at the top of the calendar to switch to another user's view. Their events load in place of yours, so you can see what's on their schedule for any date.
Feature 4
Tapping into event details
Click to learn →
Event Details
Tap any event on the calendar to open its full detail view — including time, location, RSVP status, and any notes or attachments the organizer added to the event.
