Start a 1:1 Conversation

1

Open the Teamworks Hub mobile app and tap the Messages icon.

2

Tap the compose icon (pencil) in the top right corner.

3

Search for the person's name and select them from the results.

4

Type your message and tap Send.

5

Your conversation thread opens — only you and the recipient can see this exchange.

✓ Your private message is sent. The recipient will get a push notification instantly.

Create a Group Chat

1

Open the Teamworks Hub mobile app and tap the Messages icon.

2

Tap the compose icon (pencil) and select New Group Chat.

3

Search for and add each participant. You can add teammates, coaches, or staff.

4

Give the group chat a name (e.g. "Position Coaches", "Travel Squad").

5

Type your message and tap Send — all members see the thread and can reply.

✓ Group chat created. All members are notified and can participate in the conversation.
