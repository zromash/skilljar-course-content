Branching Scenario

Your trip is built — now assign travelers to where they're staying and how they're getting around.

You're preparing traveler assignments for an upcoming away game. What do you need to set up first?

You chose

Hotel Room Assignments

1

Navigate to the Travel module, open the trip, and select the Hotel tab on the left side.

2

Select the trip leg if your trip has multiple stops with different hotels.

3

Click Add Rooms — choose the room type and quantity needed, then use the plus icon to add different room types. Click Create Rooms.

4

Drag and drop travel party members into their assigned rooms. Enter room numbers, check-in/check-out dates, master bill preferences, and any additional notes.

5

To delete a room, click the three-dot icon next to it and select Delete.

✓ Hotel assignments saved. Travelers can view their room assignments on the Hub mobile app.

Ground Transportation Assignments

1

Navigate to the Travel module, open the trip, and select the Ground tab on the left side.

2

Click Add Vehicle and enter the vehicle name — for example "Bus 1" or "Van — Coaches." Add additional vehicles as needed.

3

Drag and drop individual travel party members into their designated vehicles so everyone knows their transportation arrangement.

4

Travelers can see their ground assignment in the Hub mobile app under their trip details.

✓ Ground assignments complete. Travelers will see their bus or van assignment in the Hub mobile app when the trip is visible to them.
