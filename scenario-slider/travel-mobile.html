Walk through managing travel from the Hub mobile app.

Scenario: Your team is traveling and the itinerary changes on the road. Walk through how you'd update trip details and communicate changes to travelers directly from your phone.

Step 1 of 5
Open Hub on your phone and check the dashboard for upcoming trips.
Any trip within 7 days of its start date appears automatically on your dashboard. Tap the trip card to access all details — itinerary, notes, assignments, files, vendors, and the full travel party.
Step 2 of 5
Tap an itinerary event to edit or delete it.
Select the event you need to change, then tap the edit icon. You'll be prompted to choose whether to edit the event details or delete it entirely — useful when departure times change last minute.
Step 3 of 5
Tap the plus icon to add a new itinerary event.
The plus icon opens the add menu — create a new itinerary event, add a trip note, message travelers, or send out updated travel assignments, all without opening a laptop.
Step 4 of 5
Message travelers directly from the trip view.
Use the message travelers option in the plus menu to send a message blast to the travel party — ideal for communicating real-time changes like a delayed bus or updated meal time.
Step 5 of 5
Access trips beyond your dashboard with the three-dot icon.
Tap the three-dot icon in the bottom right corner and select the Travel module to see all current, upcoming, and past trips — not just the ones surfaced on your dashboard.

✓ You managed the full trip from your phone — updated the itinerary, added a note, and messaged the travel party without needing to open a laptop.
