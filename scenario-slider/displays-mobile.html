Walk through managing Digital Displays from your mobile device.

Scenario: You're away from your desk and need to push an urgent announcement and update a configuration on the fly. Walk through using the Digital Displays mobile interface to get it done.

Step 1 of 4
Open Digital Displays in your mobile browser.
On your phone, open a browser and navigate to app.displays.tw. Log in with your Teamworks credentials. The Digital Displays mobile interface loads with a bottom toolbar giving you access to all main features: Devices, Configurations, Announcements, Playlists, Themes, Settings, and Organizations.
Step 2 of 4
Manage devices from the Devices tab.
Tap the Devices tab to see all your connected displays. Use the pencil icon to edit a device's settings — swap its content, adjust scale, or change time zone. You can also add new devices here by scanning a QR code or setting up a virtual device.
Step 3 of 4
Edit or create a configuration from mobile.
Tap the Configurations tab to see all existing configurations. Tap the pencil icon on any configuration to open its customizable menu and make edits — changes save and push to live displays immediately. You can also create new configurations from mobile: assign a name, select a template, and save.
Step 4 of 4
Push an announcement from the Announcements tab.
Tap the Announcements tab to manage all running announcements — these appear on the ticker bar at the top of your digital displays. Edit existing announcements or tap to add a new one directly from your phone. Changes go live on all targeted displays immediately after saving.

✓ Updates pushed from mobile — your displays reflect the latest content without needing to be at a desk.
