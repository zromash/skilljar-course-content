Scenario Slider

Walk through adding a new device and launching your first digital display.

Scenario: You've set up your configuration and it's time to go live. Walk through adding the locker room TV as a device and assigning your playlist to it.

Step 1 of 4
Navigate to Devices and add a new device.
Select Devices from the navigation menu and click Add New Device. On the TV you want to connect, open a browser and navigate to displays.tw — this generates a QR code on the screen.
Step 2 of 4
Scan the QR code to link the device.
Use your phone to scan the QR code displayed on the TV. This opens the device setup page on your phone, where you can complete the configuration — no need to type anything directly on the TV.
Step 3 of 4
Configure the device settings.
Enter a Device Name (e.g., "Locker Room TV"). Set the Device Mode to Playlist, then select your playlist from the dropdown. Set the time zone and locale for accurate scheduling. Adjust Device Scale if content appears too large or small (default is 100%).
Step 4 of 4
Save and launch the display.
Review all settings and save. The TV will begin displaying your content immediately. The device appears in your Devices table where you can edit settings, swap content, or check status at any time. Toggle Skip Interaction Screen on if you don't want users to press a button before content shows.

✓ Display launched — the locker room TV is live and running your playlist.
