Scenario Slider

Walk through creating a playlist that rotates multiple configurations on a single display.

Scenario: Your lobby display needs to rotate between a schedule view, a highlights reel, and a nutrition menu throughout the day. Walk through building a playlist that combines all three configurations.

Step 1 of 4
Create the playlist and give it a name.
Select Playlists from the navigation menu and click Add New Playlist. Enter a name (e.g., "Lobby Rotation") and click Create Playlist. The customizable playlist panel opens on the right side of the screen.
Step 2 of 4
Add announcement tags and configurations.
In the Announcements field, add any tags whose announcements should run across this playlist. Then click Add configuration to pull in existing configurations from your library — add the schedule view, highlights reel, and nutrition menu one by one.
Step 3 of 4
Set the playback order and duration for each configuration.
Drag configurations into the order you want them to display, or enable Shuffle for random rotation. For each configuration, choose Timed to keep it on screen for a set duration before advancing, or Loop to repeat media a set number of times before transitioning.
Step 4 of 4
Save and assign the playlist to a device.
Click Save Changes to finalize the playlist. It's now available to assign to any device in the Devices module. When a device plays this playlist, it automatically cycles through all three configurations in the order and timing you configured.

✓ Playlist saved — the lobby display will now rotate through your schedule, highlights, and nutrition configurations automatically.
