Step through adding a trip to Teamworks Hub.

Scenario: Your team is traveling to play an away game next month. Walk through creating the trip in Hub so your travel party can see the details and you can start building out the itinerary.

Step 1 of 6
Navigate to Travel → click "Add a Trip" in the top right corner.
The Add a Trip form opens. This is where you'll configure all the core details for your new trip before adding itinerary events and assignments.
Step 2 of 6
Enter a trip name, add an image, and select the team(s).
Use the opponent name or event name as the trip name. You can add multiple teams if they're traveling together — just make sure each team has a season assigned.
Step 3 of 6
Select the season, set the start date and time, and choose the time zone.
The season ties this trip to your organizational framework. Check the end date box if the trip spans multiple days and enter the return date and time.
Step 4 of 6
Set trip visibility — choose who can see the itinerary details.
Options: No One (not ready to share yet), Specific Travelers (select individuals), or the entire travel party. You can change this at any time.
Step 5 of 6
Choose whether the trip should be viewable on the Hub mobile app.
Select Yes, viewable on mobile for the trip and itinerary to appear on travelers' dashboards. Select No if you're still building it out and aren't ready to surface it yet.
Step 6 of 6
Save as a blank trip or duplicate from a previous trip.
Use Copy From to pull details from a previous trip with a similar schedule — great for recurring opponents. Select Create Blank Trip to start fresh. Click Save to create the trip.

✓ Trip created! Your trip now appears in the Trips table. Click into it to begin adding itinerary events, your travel party, and assignments.
