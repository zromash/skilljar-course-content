Step through the scenario one decision at a time.

Scenario: You sent a message blast to the full roster yesterday. Your head coach asks: "Did everyone read it?" Walk through how you'd find out.

Step 1 of 5
Navigate to the Messages module in Hub.
This is where all sent blasts and conversations live. You'll find your blast history here.
Step 2 of 5
Open the Sent tab.
The Sent tab shows every blast you've sent, sorted by most recent. Find yesterday's blast in the list.
Step 3 of 5
Click on the blast to open its detail view.
You'll see the full message, the send time, and the complete recipient list with their read status.
Step 4 of 5
Review the read receipts column.
Each recipient shows a read or unread status. You can see exactly who has and hasn't opened the message.
Step 5 of 5
Follow up with unread recipients directly.
Click a recipient's name to open a 1:1 conversation and follow up individually — no need to blast the whole team again.

✓ You found out exactly who read the blast and followed up with those who didn't — accountability made easy.
