Walk through importing a recruit from C+R into a GM budget.

Scenario: You're building next year's budget and want to model what it looks like if a top recruit commits. Walk through pulling that recruit from Compliance + Recruiting directly into your GM budget.

Step 1 of 5
Navigate to the Budgets page.
From the GM navigation, select Budgets. This page lists all budgets you have access to — locate the budget where you want to model this recruit's financial commitment.
Step 2 of 5
Open the target budget.
Select the budget you want to import the recruit into. Recruits are imported per budget — you can add the same recruit to multiple budgets if you're running different planning scenarios.
Step 3 of 5
Click Import Recruit.
Inside the budget, select the Import Recruit button. This opens the recruit import panel connected to your C+R pipeline. If this button isn't visible, contact your administrator — the integration may need to be enabled for your account.
Step 4 of 5
Search for and select the recruit.
Use the search field to find the recruit by name. Select them from the results — their profile information pulls directly from Compliance + Recruiting, so no duplicate data entry is needed.
Step 5 of 5
Confirm and begin allocating.
Confirm the import to add the recruit to your budget. Their record now appears in the budget alongside your current roster — allocate dollars to their profile just as you would for any other athlete to begin modeling their financial impact.

✓ Recruit imported — they're now in your budget and ready for financial planning.
