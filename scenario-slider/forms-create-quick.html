Walk through building a quick form from start to finish in Teamworks Hub.

Scenario: Your staff needs to collect dietary restrictions from all athletes before an upcoming road trip. Walk through creating a Quick Form to gather this information directly in Hub.

Step 1 of 5
Navigate to Forms and open the Form Builder.
Go to the Forms module in the navigation menu. Select Create a Quick Form to open the Form Builder. The left panel shows all available question types you can add.
Step 2 of 5
Enter a title and add your first question.
Type a descriptive form title at the top. Then select a question type from the left panel — for dietary restrictions, choose Multiple Choice or Checkboxes and type in your question text and response options.
Step 3 of 5
Configure question settings and add conditional logic.
Use the right panel to mark questions as required. Add conditional logic to show follow-up questions only when relevant — for example, show an allergy detail field only if someone selects "Yes, I have allergies."
Step 4 of 5
Duplicate questions and add any additional elements.
Use Duplicate on similar questions to save time, then adjust the titles and options. You can also add images or PDFs for reference, or use the Profile Attribute type to automatically update athlete profiles based on responses.
Step 5 of 5
Save and assign the form to your team.
Select Save and Assign to proceed directly to the assignment workflow, or Save as Draft to continue editing later. Once assigned, athletes will see the form in their Needs Attention section.

✓ Quick Form built and ready to assign — dietary restrictions collected without a single email or spreadsheet.
