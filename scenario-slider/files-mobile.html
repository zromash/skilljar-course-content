Walk through creating a folder and uploading a file from the Hub mobile app.

Scenario: You're away from your desk and need to create a new folder for your team and upload a document from your phone. Walk through how to do it entirely from the Hub mobile app.

Step 1 of 5
Access the Files section from the bottom bar.
Select the folder icon on the bottom navigation bar to open Files. Tap the All tab for a full view of your folders, then select the team where you want to create the new folder.
Step 2 of 5
Tap the plus icon to create a new folder.
Select the plus icon and choose to create a new folder. Give it a descriptive name, add optional notes, and decide whether to pin it to the top of the list for quick access.
Step 3 of 5
Navigate into the folder and tap the plus icon to upload a file.
Open the folder you just created, then tap the plus icon in the bottom right corner and select Upload File. You can take a new photo, upload from your photo library, or select a file from your device.
Step 4 of 5
Configure who can view the file.
Set sharing permissions based on user type, position, status, year, or custom groups. This controls who on the team will have access to the file once it's uploaded.
Step 5 of 5
Set alert preferences and tap Upload.
Choose to pin the file to the dashboard, then select your alert preference — email, push notification, both, or none. Tap Upload to complete the process and make the file available to your team.

✓ Folder created and file uploaded from your phone — all without needing to open a laptop.
