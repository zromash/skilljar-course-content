Walk through accessing Digital Displays and creating your first theme.

Scenario: You're setting up Digital Displays for your facility for the first time. Walk through accessing the platform from Hub and creating a branded theme with your organization's colors and logo.

Step 1 of 5
Access Digital Displays from Teamworks Hub.
From your Hub dashboard, select the App Switcher in the navigation menu, then choose Digital Displays. This opens the Digital Displays platform directly from your existing Hub account — no separate login required.
Step 2 of 5
Navigate to Themes and create a new theme.
Select Themes from the navigation menu and click New Theme. The theme configuration panel opens on the right side of the screen — this is where you'll define the visual identity of your displays.
Step 3 of 5
Set the theme name and logo.
Enter a name for your theme. For the logo, choose to use your organization's logo from Hub or upload a custom one. The preview on the left updates live so you can see exactly how it will look on a display.
Step 4 of 5
Configure colors and theme contrast.
Your organization's default colors are pre-filled for background and accent. Click any color box to customize — adjust background, accent, and announcement bar colors independently. Finally, select your Theme Contrast level (light or dark) to ensure text is readable against your chosen background.
Step 5 of 5
Review and create the theme.
Check the live preview on the left — confirm the colors, logo, and contrast look correct. Click Create Theme to save. Your theme appears in the Themes table and can be edited anytime using the pencil icon in its row.

✓ Theme created — your organization's visual identity is ready to apply to configurations and displays.
