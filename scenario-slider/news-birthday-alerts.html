Walk through setting up birthday alerts so coaches and staff never miss an athlete's birthday.

Scenario: You want coaches to be automatically reminded when any athlete on their roster has a birthday. Walk through creating a birthday alert that shows up pinned to their Hub dashboard on the day it happens.

Step 1 of 4
Navigate to Birthday Alerts in the News tab.
Go to the News tab and select Birthday Alerts from the navigation. This section shows all configured birthday alerts for your organization. Click Create Alert to set up a new one.
Step 2 of 4
Name the alert and select whose birthdays to track.
Enter a title for the alert (e.g., "Athlete Birthdays"). Under Generate Birthday Alerts For, select the user types whose birthdays should trigger the alert — for example, Athletes. You can include multiple user types in a single alert.
Step 3 of 4
Configure who can see the birthday alerts.
Under Birthday Alerts Visible To, select which users will see the birthday notification pinned to their dashboard — for example, Coaches or Administration. This controls visibility so only the right people are notified.
Step 4 of 4
Activate the alert and confirm setup.
Click Add Birthday Alert to activate. The alert will now automatically generate a pinned news post on each user's birthday based on your settings. Use the three-dot icon to edit or delete the alert anytime, and the dropdown to review current settings.

✓ Birthday alert configured — coaches will see a pinned reminder on their dashboard every time an athlete has a birthday.
