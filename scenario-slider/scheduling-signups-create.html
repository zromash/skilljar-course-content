Walk through creating a calendar sign-up for athlete appointments.

Scenario: Your sports medicine staff is offering 30-minute individual recovery consultations. Walk through setting up a sign-up so athletes can book their own appointments.

Step 1 of 5
Navigate to the Sign-Ups tab and create a new sign-up.
Go to the Calendar section and click the Sign-Ups tab at the top. Click Create Sign-Up to open the setup form. Give the sign-up a clear name — athletes will see this when they browse available appointments.
Step 2 of 5
Configure slot duration, buffer time, and limits.
Set the appointment duration (e.g., 30 minutes). Add a buffer time between appointments to give staff a break. Set a per-person limit to control how many appointments one person can book, and set the total slots available per time window.
Step 3 of 5
Set availability dates and a sign-up deadline.
Define the date range when appointments are available. Then set a sign-up deadline — a cutoff date before which all bookings must be made. This prevents last-minute reservations that staff can't accommodate.
Step 4 of 5
Assign a resource calendar to the sign-up.
In the Resource Calendar section, assign the staff member or room whose availability this sign-up reflects. The resource calendar drives which time slots show as open — booked slots are automatically blocked when the calendar is full.
Step 5 of 5
Publish and monitor via the appointment overview.
Click Save to publish the sign-up. Athletes can now see and book appointments from the Resources section in their mobile app. Track who has booked and how many slots remain from the Appointment Overview in the Sign-Ups tab.

✓ Sign-up is live — athletes can now self-book their recovery consultations and you can monitor all appointments in one place.
