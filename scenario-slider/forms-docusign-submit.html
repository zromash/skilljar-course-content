Walk through submitting a form for DocuSign conversion in Teamworks Hub.

Scenario: Your organization has an athlete waiver that requires a legally binding signature. Walk through how to submit a Quick Form for DocuSign conversion so it can be sent as an official e-signature document.

Step 1 of 5
Open the Forms module and go to Templates.
Navigate to the Forms module and select the Templates tab. This shows all your saved Quick Form templates. Locate the form you want to convert to a DocuSign document.
Step 2 of 5
Select Submit for DocuSign Conversion from the three-dot menu.
Click the three-dot menu next to the Quick Form template and select Submit for DocuSign Conversion. This begins the process of converting the form into a DocuSign-compatible format.
Step 3 of 5
Review the form and add any DocuSign signature fields.
In the DocuSign editor, review the form layout and add signature fields, initials, and date fields where required. This step ensures the document is structured correctly for e-signature collection.
Step 4 of 5
Submit the conversion request for processing.
Once the signature fields are configured, select Submit to send the conversion request. The form will be processed and added to your DocuSign Forms templates, where it can be assigned like any other form.
Step 5 of 5
Confirm the DocuSign Form is available in Templates.
Return to the Templates tab and use the DocuSign Forms filter to confirm the converted form appears. It's now ready to be assigned to athletes or external recipients who need to provide a verified signature.

✓ Form submitted for DocuSign conversion — now ready to collect legally binding e-signatures from your team.
