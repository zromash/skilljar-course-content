Walk through adding the Digital Displays widget to your phone's home screen.

Scenario: You want one-tap access to Digital Displays from your phone without opening a browser and searching every time. Walk through adding it as a home screen widget in four steps.

Step 1 of 4
Navigate to the Digital Displays URL in your mobile browser.
Open your mobile browser and go to app.teamworks.tw. Make sure you're logged in — the Digital Displays interface should be visible before proceeding to add the shortcut.
Step 2 of 4
Tap the Share button at the bottom of the toolbar.
Tap the Share button (the box with an arrow icon) at the bottom of your browser's toolbar. This opens the system share sheet with a list of actions you can take with the current page.
Step 3 of 4
Select "Add to Home Screen" and confirm the name.
Scroll through the share sheet and tap Add to Home Screen. A dialog appears where you can confirm or update the name for the widget — keep it something clear like "Digital Displays" so it's easy to find on your home screen.
Step 4 of 4
Tap Add to complete the setup.
Tap Add in the top right corner of the dialog. The Digital Displays widget is now on your home screen. From now on, tap the widget for instant one-tap access to the mobile browser — no bookmarks or manual URL entry required.

✓ Widget added — Digital Displays is now one tap away from your home screen.
