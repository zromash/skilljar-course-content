Scenario Slider

Walk through customizing a configuration for your locker room display.

Scenario: You've created an L-Shape with Media configuration for the main locker room. Walk through adding a game countdown, connecting the team calendar, adding a media loop, and saving.

Step 1 of 5
Set the theme and link announcement tags.
Open your configuration from the Configurations tab. In the Basic Setup section, confirm the name, then select your Theme from the dropdown — this applies your organization's colors and logo. Optionally add announcement tags to display relevant announcements on this screen.
Step 2 of 5
Add an event countdown.
In the Event Countdown section, enter an event title, start date, and time. For home or away games, upload an opponent badge and position it on the left or right side of the display. You can add multiple countdowns at once to build out your full season schedule.
Step 3 of 5
Connect the team calendar.
In the Schedule section, select the Hub calendar to display. Toggle Show Next Day Events if you want tomorrow's schedule instead of today's, or enable Hide Past Events to only show upcoming activities and prevent the loop from showing stale data.
Step 4 of 5
Add media and info cards.
In the Media section, upload images or videos (or paste a YouTube URL). Multiple items cycle through at set durations. In the Cards section, add text-based reminders with a title, content, and optional image — and schedule each card to only show within a specific date range.
Step 5 of 5
Save the configuration.
Review all sections — theme, countdown, schedule, media, and cards. Click Save Changes to finalize. The configuration is now ready to be added to a playlist or assigned directly to a device. Changes take effect on displays immediately after saving.

✓ Configuration saved — the locker room display will show your game countdown, team schedule, and media loop.
