Walk through creating and publishing a news post in Teamworks Hub.

Scenario: Your team has a last-minute schedule change for tomorrow's practice. Walk through creating a pinned announcement news post to make sure everyone sees it immediately.

Step 1 of 5
Open the news post creation flow.
From your dashboard, click Post News in the actions bar at the top — or navigate to the News tab and click Create Post. Enter a title for your post and toggle Pin to Dashboard on to keep it visible for your team.
Step 2 of 5
Choose the post type and add your content.
Select Announcement for internal communication, or External Link to redirect users to an outside resource. For an announcement, upload an optional image and write your message in the content field. Use the right panel preview to see how it will look to recipients.
Step 3 of 5
Configure visibility to target the right recipients.
In the Visibility section, click the three-dot icon to choose who can see the post — filter by user type, custom group, position, status, or year. Click Select after adding recipients. This controls exactly who the post appears for on their dashboard.
Step 4 of 5
Set publish time, expiration, and alert preference.
Choose to publish immediately or schedule it for a future time. Optionally set an expiration date so the post auto-removes when it's no longer relevant. Then decide whether to send a Teamworks alert notification — email, push, or none.
Step 5 of 5
Review and create the post.
Review all settings — title, content, visibility, and timing. Click Create Post to publish. The post will appear in the News tab and on the dashboards of all selected recipients. If pinned, it stays at the top of their dashboard feed.

✓ News post created and pinned — your team will see the practice update front and center when they open Hub.
