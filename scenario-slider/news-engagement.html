Scenario Slider

Walk through tracking news post engagement and following up with recipients who haven't viewed it.

Scenario: You posted a critical policy update yesterday. Walk through checking who has seen it and sending reminders to anyone who hasn't opened it yet.

Step 1 of 4
Navigate to the News tab and open the engagement report.
Go to the News tab to view all your published posts. Find the post you want to analyze and click the three-dot icon in the Actions column. Select View Post Engagement to open the analytics report.
Step 2 of 4
Review who has viewed and clicked the post.
The engagement report lists all assigned recipients with clear indicators showing whether each user has viewed or clicked the post. Identify anyone who hasn't engaged — these are the users who need a follow-up.
Step 3 of 4
Send individual or bulk reminders.
To remind a specific user, click Send Alert in their row. To notify everyone who hasn't opened the post at once, click Send Alert at the top of the list and confirm the bulk send. Recipients receive an alert notification prompting them to view the post.
Step 4 of 4
Use engagement data to inform future communication.
Review overall engagement rates across your posts to understand what content resonates most. Low engagement on a post may signal it needs a better title, a pinned position, or a more targeted recipient list. Use these insights to improve your next announcement.

✓ Engagement reviewed and reminders sent — you now have full visibility into who has seen your critical update.
