Checklist Simulator

Walk through sending a message blast on web. Check off each step as you go.

Scenario: You need to remind your entire roster about tomorrow's 7am practice. Send a message blast to the full team from your desktop.

✓
Step 1Navigate to the Messages module in Hub.
✓
Step 2Select New Message Blast in the top right.
✓
Step 3Choose your message type — Teamworks Message or Email.
✓
Step 4Add your recipients — select a team, group, or individual users.
✓
Step 5Compose your message in the text field. Attach a file if needed.
✓
Step 6Optionally, set a scheduled send time for a future date and time.
✓
Step 7Review everything and select Send — your blast goes out to all recipients.

✓ Blast sent! Your team will receive a push notification immediately.
